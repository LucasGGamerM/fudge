This app is a reimplementation of Fujifilm's WiFi app. Expect bugs! There are no promises that it will be better than the official app.

Troubleshooting

  • Your camera's WiFi menu should be accessible from the 'Fn' button on the top of the body.
  • On newer cameras, it's available from 'PLAY BACK MENU' -> 'WIRELESS COMMUNICATION'
  • Bluetooth is not supported yet. That's on the roadmap!

Android might be disconnecting you from the Fuji WiFi access point since it has no WiFi. (This is an Android feature) You should get a notification that can allow you to stay on the network.
